Gallipoli and Salento

Sea, history and traditions in the heart of Puglia.

Discover Gallipoli

Gallipoli is one of the most fascinating destinations in Salento, famous for its crystal-clear sea, golden beaches and its beautiful historic centre built on an island connected to the mainland.

Staying at La Suite Gallipoli allows you to experience the authentic atmosphere of this wonderful town, discovering its culture, food and local traditions.

The Historic Centre

Walking through the narrow streets of the old town means stepping into the history of Gallipoli, among ancient churches, historic buildings, artisan shops and beautiful sea views.

The Beaches of Gallipoli

The famous coastline of Gallipoli, including Baia Verde, Punta della Suina and Lido San Giovanni, offers crystal-clear waters and breathtaking scenery.

Discover Salento

Gallipoli is ideally located to explore other beautiful destinations in Salento, including Lecce, Otranto, Santa Maria di Leuca and the charming villages of the countryside.
